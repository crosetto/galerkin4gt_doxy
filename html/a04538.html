template<ushort_t ... P>
struct gdl::parametric_space< order< P ... > >

class holding the parametric space

The parametric space is depending only on the order of the basis, it will be mapped to the deformed configuration by the finite element transformation (thus depending on the position of the knots on the deformed grid)
